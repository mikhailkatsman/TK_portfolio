Hello,

I am a self-taught commercial portrait and fashion photographer based in London. My passion for photography ignited while capturing street shots, but I soon discovered my fascination with human faces and beauty, which led me to focus on portraiture.

My signature style is clean and minimalist with a strong emphasis on storytelling. Every shot is carefully curated, aiming to capture the true essence of my subjects. My goal is to create compelling and evocative imagery that tells a story and connects with the viewer. I am deeply committed to my craft and approach each project with a high level of professionalism and creativity.
